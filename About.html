About Me



JOHN SHERLOUN ESPERO
🎨 Passionate Graphic Designer | Specialization in Logo design | Free-hand Artist

Who Am I?
"I’m John Sherloun, a dedicated graphic designer with a flair for transforming ideas into captivating visuals. My journey began by coincidence, as I was just starting to learn video editing. For me, logo design is much easier than video editing, but creating and building ideas is the harder part. I believe it’s up to you to push yourself to the limit to create IDEALISTIC AND UNIQUE designs."

What Drives Me?
🌟 Creativity: I thrive on pushing boundaries and thinking outside the box. Whether it’s a logo, a banners, or a social media campaign, I love infusing each project with fresh, innovative ideas.
🎯 Purpose: Design isn’t just about aesthetics; it’s about solving problems. I’m passionate about creating designs that resonate with the audience, convey a message, and drive results.
🌈 Color Obsessed: Colors have a language of their own. I’m fascinated by their psychology and how they evoke emotions.

My Superpowers:
🔍 Attention to Detail: Pixel-perfect precision is my mantra. I obsess over alignment, typography, and that elusive white space.
🎨 Versatility: From minimalist elegance to bold and quirky, I adapt my style to suit each project’s unique needs.
🚀 Collaboration: I thrive in creative partnerships. Let’s brainstorm and bring visions to life together.
